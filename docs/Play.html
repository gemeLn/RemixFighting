How to Play

  1. Choose one of the three characters from the character selection screen. To select characters, first click on the screen and player 1 should use the keys A and D to choose which character to play with and player 2 should use the arrow keys. Player 1 should press M to confirm their selection and Player 2 should press E to confirm their selection.

  2. Once characters are selected press the space bar to start.

  3. Players should fight each other for four minutes. The yellow bars indicate the health and the bar below indicates how close the player is to using their special. The moves available to the characters are found on their character profiles pages.

  4. After the players fight one will be declared the victor.

The Keyboard

Player 1 uses the blue keys. Player 2 uses the purple keys.
