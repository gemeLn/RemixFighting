Polar Bear

  The polar bear was once exploring his homeland of Antarctica and stumbled upon the Ice House by accident while looking for food. The Ice House's master penguin looked mighty delicious to him and the polar bear attacked. The master penguin immediately beat the polar bear and the polar bear was so amazed he asked to be a student under the master penguin. The penguin taught him everything and the polar bear grew to rival the penguin in strength. Even though the polar bear was slow, his strength allowed him to cause maximum damage and he soon gained the ability at the Ice House to summon the power of the ice and manipulate it into weapons.

The Moves

The Polar Bear is the second character that we developed. This is the slowest character, but it packs a punch. This character has nine moves including a special that summons 3 waves of 10 ice shards from the ground.
