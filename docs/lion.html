Lion

The Lion grew up in Africa feeling that his life was missing something. He fought all the members of his pride and came out on top. The lion still was unsatisfied so he travelled around the world and challenged some of the strongest and powerful animals to a fight and won. One day the lion heard of the Ice House that was located in Antarctica. He heard that the greatest fighters were trained there so he spent years building endurance to the cold and finally set out to find the Ice House. After a journey of two and a half years he found the Ice House and challenged their two greatest fighters, the penguin and the polar bear. The lion brought a style of fighting never seen before to the Ice House. He was faster than the penguin and polar bear and was able to summon animals to help him fight.

The Moves
