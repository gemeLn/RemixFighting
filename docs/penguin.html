Penguin

  Once upon a time there was a lost penguin in search of the meaning of life. He travelled across vast swatches of land and finally found the Ice House. In the Ice House the penguin learned many fighting techniques and became one of the most formidable fighters. One of his great talents is the ability to fight in the air. He also learned to rain down icicles upon the heads of his opponents. As the penguin ninja he became one of masters at the Ice House.

The Moves

The penguin is the first character that we developed. This character's Hurtbox is smaller than the others as it is a small animal and it is the second fastest character available. It has nine moves including the special, which is the ability to throw down 7 icicles on their opponents.
